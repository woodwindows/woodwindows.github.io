Description

This page describes the design criteria and calculations for deciding on the layout of a mortice and tenon joint when used to join the corners of frames where the material has rebates and a wedged, through tenon will be used. Frames of this type may be used in the construction of windows and doors or for other purposes.

Imagine looking at the side of the top-left corner of a window frame: the rebate is in the bottom right corner.
